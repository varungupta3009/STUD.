INTODUCTION TO DATA SCIENCE

Course Content:

  1. Introduction to Data Science:Motivating examples and scope, Data visualization concept,Visualization data:Bar Charts,Line charts,Histogram etc.
  2. Random Variables:Random variable,expectation,function of random variable,Probability distributition: Discrete distributions , Continous distribution.
  3. Sampling:Sampling concept, The Central Limit Theorem and Application estimation,Point Estimation for population mean and proportion,interval Estimates,confidence intervals,P-values.
  4. Hypothesis and inference:Hypothesis testing for means and proportions,Distribution free test,Chi-squared test and power of test.
  5. Simple linear Regression:Building the regression model,prediction using regression models.
